-----------------------------------------------------------
FONTLOG for Thold
-----------------------------------------------------------

PROJECT DESCRIPTION
Thold is a handwriting typeface. It was originally created using Calligraphr and 
subsequently refined with FontForge to ensure correct metadata 
and compliance with open-source licensing standards.

The typeface features a bold and quickly written style, 
making it ideal for personal notes, invitations, or digital 
scrapbooking.

INFORMATION FOR DESIGNERS
If you wish to modify or contribute to this font, please refer 
to the terms of the SIL Open Font License (OFL).
